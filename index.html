Visit Dhaka

Explore Rajshahi, the cleanest city in Asia and the capital of the
Rajshahi Division in Bangladesh. Renowned for its pure silk, mangoes, and lychees, this vibrant 'silk city' offers ancient capitals, mosques,
shrines, and temples to discover.

Top three activities to do at Rajshahi

Enchanting Nights in Rajshahi

Experience the mesmerizing transformation of Rajshahi at night as illuminated streets captivate residents and visitors alike, guiding them through the city's vibrant charm.

Serene Sunrise at T Bandh

Witness the spectacular sunrise over the Padma River at T Dam or I Dam, offering breathtaking views of this mighty river.

Discover Rajshahi University

Stroll through Rajshahi University's picturesque campus, just five kilometers from the city center, and recharge amidst its serene beauty and fresh air.
